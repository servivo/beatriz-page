I have been teaching future teachers on didactics and classroom management since 2018. I hold a certificate on University Didactics (200+ hours of work) recognized by the Ministry of Education of North-Rhine Westfalia. I strive to develop interactive classes in which my students are encouraged to experiment themselves in the role of a teacher, while building social competences and soft skills.
